Ratatouille

Description

This delicious recipe was inspired from the best Disney Pixar film, "Ratatouille". It's masterfully put together by combining thinly sliced vegetables and topped with herbs.

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma Tomatoes
  • 3 Bell Peppers, Roasted and Seeded
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 Yellow Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Stock
  • 2 Teaspoons Herbs de Provence
  • 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil, Divided
  • Small Bunch Fresh Parsley
  • 3 Basin Leaves, Torn
  • 1 Japanese Eggplant
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 1 Yellow Squash
  • 2 Sprigs of Rosemary
  • 1 Single Chive, for Garnish
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Blanch and peel all your tomatoes. Then in a large pot add 2 tbsp olive oil, chili flakes, garlic, three of your tomatoes, onion, garlic and your bell peppers.
  2. Sauté veg until soft on medium-medium high heat Season to taste. Once soft, add Herbes de Provence, parsley, basil and veg stock. Remove from heat, blend then add back to heat and reduce.
  3. Thinly slice egg plant, zucchini, squash and the rest of your tomatoes. Finely chop your rosemary.
  4. In a cast iron or baking pan, add a thin layer of your sauce. Shingle your veg and layer onto your surface. Top with a drizzle of olive oil, finely chopped rosemary and a healthy pinch of salt.
  5. Cover with parchment paper and bake in a 225°F oven for about 80-90 minutes. You want your veg to be tender, not mushy. Plate and serve.
